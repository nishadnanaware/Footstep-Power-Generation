# Footstep-Power-Generation
Overview

This project generates electrical energy from human footsteps using piezoelectric sensors and stores it for later use. The system also provides controlled power output using RFID authentication.

---

Objectives

* Convert mechanical energy → electrical energy
* Store energy in rechargeable battery
* Provide controlled access to stored energy
* Demonstrate renewable energy harvesting

---
Working Principle

1. Foot pressure applied on piezo sensors
2. Sensors produce AC voltage
3. Bridge rectifier converts AC → DC
4. Boost converter stabilizes voltage
5. Energy stored in battery
6. RFID authentication enables output
7. Relay supplies power to load

---

Components Used

* Arduino Uno
* Piezoelectric sensors (3×3 array)
* Bridge rectifier
* Boost converter (XL6009)
* 12V battery pack
* RFID reader (RC522)
* Relay module
* LCD display (16×2, I2C)
* Voltmeter & Ammeter
